Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how Qard handles your data.

Effective Date: January 27, 2022

Privacy at a Glance

Qard is built with a privacy-first design. All data processing is performed locally on your device. Your personal information never leaves your iPhone or iPad unless you explicitly choose to share it — and even then, we never have access to it.

Key Point: Qard does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device, under your control.

Information We Collect

Qard does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on-device. You enter your contact details — name, phone number, email, website, and address — and all of that information remains stored locally in your device's storage and is used only to generate QR codes and populate the app's interface.

What About Apple Wallet Passes?

If you choose to create an Apple Wallet pass for a store membership card, the membership information is sent temporarily to Apple's servers to generate the pass, as required by the Wallet system. This is a standard part of Apple Wallet functionality and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Qard does not store this data.

How Your Data Is Used

Since Qard does not collect your data, there is nothing to use or share. To be explicit:

  • No data collection. We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics tied to your identity.
  • No third-party sharing. Your contact information is never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • No advertising. The app contains no ads and no ad-tracking frameworks.
  • No account required. Qard does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Qard is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and FileManager container storage for app groups). The data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Qard uses the following third-party services, each with strict privacy practices:

  • Firebase — Used solely for the Apple Wallet pass generation feature. When you choose to create a Wallet pass, your vCard contact data is temporarily uploaded to Firebase to generate the pass file. This data is not used for any other purpose and is not stored permanently. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Used for in-app purchase management. No personal contact information is shared. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Qard is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we do not collect information from any user, there is no risk of unknowingly collecting data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at apps@maadotaa.com.
